About DormCart

DormCart is a campus marketplace where students can buy, sell, and exchange items safely and easily.

Our Mission

To make student life simpler by creating a trusted hub for exchanging everyday essentials within the campus community.

How It Works

  • Sign up with your campus email.
  • List items you want to sell or browse items others posted.
  • Connect with fellow students and agree on a safe exchange method.
